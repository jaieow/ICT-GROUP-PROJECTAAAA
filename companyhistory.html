Want to know how our humble little tea shop started?

Well, it all started one rainy night, our founder was staying on school grounds late for some journalism practice, and suddenly, had a craving for something to drink, so he went out, and noticed that while there were a few shops open, none were close enough to the grounds, so then, he had a thought, "What if I could start one for students like me, who stay late into the night in the grounds?" And so, the next day, he shared this idea with a few of his friends, and along with them and their parents, were able to make this humble tea shop, just outside the gates of Taguig Science High School!
